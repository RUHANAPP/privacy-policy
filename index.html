Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

RUHAN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Location Data: Used solely to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Prayer Time Preferences: Calculation method, notifications, and display settings
  • Quran Reading Progress: Bookmarks and last read position
  • Notification Settings: Prayer alerts and Ramadan reminders
  • Cycle Tracker Data: Stored exclusively on your device, never transmitted
  • App Settings: Language, theme, and ad-free purchase status

2. Third-Party Advertising

Our free version displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These services may collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, model, OS version, advertising ID
  • IP Address: Used to estimate general location for relevant ads
  • Ad Interaction Data: Ads you view or interact with
  • Crash Logs & Performance Data: For SDK improvement

You can remove all ads with a one-time in-app purchase. Ad-free users have no advertising data collected.

Google Privacy Policy

3. App Tracking Transparency

When you first use the App, you will be asked whether you consent to personalized advertising through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.

  • If you ALLOW tracking: You may receive personalized ads
  • If you DENY tracking: You will receive non-personalized ads

You can change this preference anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. In-App Purchases

All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store.

  • We do NOT collect or have access to your payment information
  • Your purchase history is managed by Apple
  • Refunds are subject to Apple's refund policy

5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following services to provide content:

  • AlQuran.cloud – Quran text and translations
  • MP3Quran.net – Surah audio recitations
  • EveryAyah.com – Verse-by-verse audio
  • PrayTimes.org – Adhan audio files

These services only deliver content to your device and do not receive personal data from you.

6. Location Services

The App requests location access to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times
  • Determine Qibla direction

Your location data is processed on your device and can be disabled anytime. You may also manually enter your location.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all personal data is stored locally:

  • Uninstalling the App deletes all associated data
  • You can clear App data through your device settings
  • Reset your Advertising ID in Settings → Privacy → Advertising

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for users of all ages and contains Islamic religious content suitable for families. We do not knowingly serve personalized ads to children under 13.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the App)
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Disable location services
